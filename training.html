Free Cohort Weekly Milestones

Week 1

Logic & Setup

Setting up compiler/IDEs, understanding variables, memory compilation steps, and operators syntax.

Week 2

Conditionals & Loops

Mastering if-else conditional branches, switch-cases, for/while loops, nested loops patterns, and dry runs.

Week 3

Functions & Arrays

Creating modular functions, parameters scope, 1D/2D arrays allocation, indexing, and linear operations.

Week 4

Git & Capstones

Learning Git init, commits, cloning from GitHub, pushing code, and building/presenting cohort capstone project.

Frequently Asked Questions (Free Training)

Is the graduation certificate free?
Yes, absolutely. The digital certificate of completion is issued to all candidates who attend classes regularly and finish at least 80% of weekly coding assignments. There is no fee for generating or verifying the certificate.
What are the class timings?
Classes are scheduled in the evenings (usually between 6:00 PM and 8:30 PM IST) to ensure that students can attend without missing their regular school or college classes. Detail links are shared on our Telegram group.
Will I get doubt support?
Yes, we provide community-based doubt support on Discord/Telegram. Teaching assistants and peer mentors help debug code snippets and logic errors.
